Level 1 — Driver Registration Training

1. Help driver download YENE Driver App.
2. Click Sign Up and enter driver cellphone number.
3. Wait for OTP and insert it.
4. Enter full name, email, and create password.

Documents to prepare:
• Driver licence front and back
• ID front and back (if available)
• Vehicle front and back images
• Vehicle disc / registration / insurance

Important:
• Yene charges 7% per ride as service fee
• New drivers get 14 day trial
• Agent must copy driver code like PAR.... from profile and use it in network registration

Level 2 — Client Registration Training

1. Help client download the Yene rider app.
2. Sign up with cellphone number and OTP.
3. Enter full name, gender, email, and password.
4. Client can use the app immediately without approval.
5. Open client profile, go to Invite a Friend, copy the code, and use that when registering the client in your network.
